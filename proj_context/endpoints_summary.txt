# dineops_backend - Key Endpoints Summary

## Authentication
POST /api/accounts/token/ - Obtain JWT tokens
POST /api/accounts/token/refresh/ - Refresh access token

## Tenants (Superuser only)
GET/POST /api/accounts/tenants/ - List/Create tenants
GET/PUT/DELETE /api/accounts/tenants/{id}/ - Retrieve/Update/Delete specific tenant

## Users
GET/POST /api/accounts/users/ - List/Create users
GET/PUT/DELETE /api/accounts/users/{id}/ - Retrieve/Update/Delete specific user

## Tables
GET/POST /api/accounts/tables/ - List/Create tables
GET/PUT/DELETE /api/accounts/tables/{id}/ - Retrieve/Update/Delete specific table

## Food Items
GET/POST /api/foods/fooditems/ - List/Create food items
GET/PUT/DELETE /api/foods/fooditems/{id}/ - Retrieve/Update/Delete specific food item

## Orders
GET/POST /api/orders/orders/ - List/Create orders
GET/PUT/DELETE /api/orders/orders/{id}/ - Retrieve/Update/Delete specific order

## Rooms (Hotel feature)
GET/POST /api/hotel/rooms/ - List/Create rooms
GET/PUT/DELETE /api/hotel/rooms/{id}/ - Retrieve/Update/Delete specific room

## Bookings (Hotel feature)
GET/POST /api/hotel/bookings/ - List/Create bookings
GET/PUT/DELETE /api/hotel/bookings/{id}/ - Retrieve/Update/Delete specific booking

## Bills
GET/POST /api/billing/bills/ - List/Create bills
GET/PUT/DELETE /api/billing/bills/{id}/ - Retrieve/Update/Delete specific bill

## Payments
GET/POST /api/billing/payments/ - List/Create payments
GET/PUT/DELETE /api/billing/payments/{id}/ - Retrieve/Update/Delete specific payment
