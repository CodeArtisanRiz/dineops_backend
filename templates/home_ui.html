Welcome to DineOps

DineOps is a comprehensive multi-tenant management system designed for restaurants and hotels. Our platform offers a range of features to streamline operations and enhance customer experiences.

  • User authentication with secure token management
  • Robust multi-tenant architecture for data isolation
  • Efficient CRUD operations for managing tenants, users, tables, food items, orders, rooms, bookings, services, and billing
  • Customizable dashboards and reports for insightful analytics
  • Seamless integration with third-party services
